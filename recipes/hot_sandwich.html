Hot sandwich

Description

One of the simplest cooked dishes you could make. Very versatile, great when you have to throw something together from leftover ingredients. Best enjoyed while still hot!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Basically any kind of cold meat you have at home
  • Some kind of vegetables (optional)(I recommend onions)
  • Some kind of sauce (optional)
  • Cheese (mandatory!)

Steps

  1. Slice the bread.
  2. Butter the bread.
  3. Put your cold meat on the bread.
  4. (optional) Put your vegetables on the bread.
  5. Grate your cheese on the bread (mandatory!).
  6. Put another slice of bread on top.
  7. Put the sandwich in the oven or a sandwich maker and cook until the bread is brown.
  8. (optional) Put your sauces on the bread.
