Hamburger

Description

The classic that everyone knows. Originally from Germany, but it has spread worlwide and taken root in the cuisine of multiple nations, including the USA. Not the most difficult of dishes, but always a great option when you are hungry!

Ingredients

  • Hamburger Bun
  • Ground Meat
  • Preferred vegetables (e.g. onion, tomato, cucumber, pepper, salad)
  • Preferred sauces (e.g. mustard, ketchup, mayo, barbecue, chimichurri)

Steps

  1. Cut the bun in half.
  2. Knead the meat into shapely meat buns.
  3. Prepare the vegetables.
  4. Put the meat buns into a frying pan, cook until it reaches your preferred rarity.
  5. Put the cooked meat into the bottom half of the hamburger bun.
  6. Place the vegetables on top of the meat in an appetizing fashion.
  7. Pour your preferred sauces on top of the vegetables.
  8. Place the top half of the hamburger bun on the very top.
