Glass of Water

Description

The best dish for quenching thirst. Easy to make, and cheap to boot! Recommended for all ages and types.

Ingredients

  • Cup (preferably glass, but plastic is fine)
  • A working tap with clean water

Steps

  1. Take the cup into your hand and place it under the tap.
  2. Turn on the tap while the cup is underneath.
  3. Wait until the cup is filled up to 80% (don't fill more than 90% in order to avoid risk of spilling!).
  4. Turn off the tap.
  5. Your glass of water is ready!
